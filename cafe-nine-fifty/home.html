Welcome to Cafe 9:50

Where Every Sip and Slice Is a Moment to Savor

At Cafe 9:50, time slows down. The bold aroma of handpicked coffee beans mingles with the warmth of stone-baked, gourmet pizzas — crafted to delight, served with care. This is more than a café; it’s a thoughtfully curated space where taste meets tranquility, and every detail is designed to elevate your everyday pause.

Established in 2025, Cafe 9:50 is more than a place to dine — it’s where conversations brew as richly as the coffee, and every slice of pizza carries the warmth of home. We take pride in curating an inviting atmosphere where moments are shared, ideas take shape, and quality is evident in every cup we serve and every oven-fresh bite we craft.

Meet Our Founders

Viki and Nivedita

Viki and Nivedita met in a coffee shop, where their shared love for great coffee and meaningful connections sparked a partnership that would change their lives. Their passion for the craft of coffee and the community it brings together led them to create Brewfye.

With years of experience and a deep understanding of what makes a café special, they set out to build a space that reflects their values—quality, warmth, and connection. Their mission is simple: to offer an unforgettable experience, one cup at a time.

Proudly originating in a former 19th century watch house on London's famous Barondess Street.
